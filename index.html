Mike Tyson

Everyone has a plan 'till they get punched in the mouth.

Mike Tyson's life:

  • 1986 Tyson knocks out Trevor Berbick, winning the WBC heavyweight title to become the youngest heavyweight champion in history at age 20.
  • 1987 Tyson defeats James "Bonecrusher" Smith to win the WBA heavyweight title and later Tony Tucker to win the IBF heavyweight crown to unify the world titles.
  • 1988 Tyson weds actress Robin Givens in New York. 8 months later Givens files for divorce amidst claims of domestic violence. In October, Tyson announces his partnership with controversial promoter Don King.
  • 1990 Tyson is knocked out by James "Buster" Douglas in the 10th round and loses his world heavyweight title.
  • 1991 Desiree Washington files a complaint with police accusing Tyson of rape.
  • 1992 Tyson is found guilty of rape and sentenced to 10 years in prison.
  • 1995 Tyson is released from the Indiana Youth Center near Plainfield, Indiana. Begins his comeback by knocking out Peter McNeeley in Las Vegas and Buster Mathis Jnr. in Philadelphia.
  • 1996 Knocks out Frank Bruno and Bruce Seldon, both in Las Vegas, both fights gaining Tyson WBA heavyweight titles. Later loses title to Evander Holyfield.
  • 1997 Tyson bites Evander Holyfield and is disqualified in their rematch. Two weeks later Tyson's boxing license is revoked and he is fined $3 million.
  • 1998 Tyson Files a $100 million lawsuit against Don King, accusing the promoter of cheating him out of tens of millions of dollars.
  • 2000 In a bout with Lou Savarese, Tyson knocks the referee down in order to keep punching Savarese after the bout was stopped. Tyson was fined $187,500.
  • 2002 A press conference to announce the Tyson - Lennox Lewis fight breaks into an all-out brawl. Tyson later admits to having bitten Lewis during the melee. In June, Tyson is knocked out by Lewis.
  • 2003 Tyson is divorced from his wife Monica, with whom he has two children. Tyson files for bankruptcy.
  • 2004 In an interview, Tyson announces that since declaring bankruptcy: "I've got nowhere to live. I've been literally sleeping in shelters. Unsavory characters are giving me money and I'm taking it. I need it".
  • 2005 Tyson fails to come out for the seventh round against Kevin McBride. Soon after he announces his retirement from boxing.
  • 2006 Tyson is arrested in Arizona, on suspicion of driving under the influence and felony drug possession.
  • 2007 Pleads guilty to possession of narcotics and driving under the influence. He is convicted in November.
  • 2008 James Toback's film "Tyson" is shown at the Cannes Film Festival.
